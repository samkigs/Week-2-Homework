AKAN NAMES

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently ni Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian Day Names.

Males

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Females

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
